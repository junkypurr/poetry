Preface

When I think of poetry, I think of calm. I think about 3 AM adventures through belongings you forgot you still had. The things that I’ve learned in this class is insane, to be honest. I’ve gotten enough creative ability to feel comfortable in my work as a writer, and overall as an artist. The process that I have for writing is simple. I want every single piece of poetry to be significant to me. So, that’s where I start. I always want to pick a topic that I can easily write about, without it being too hard to find words without it seeming like a stretch.

By Myself

When I’m by myself

And I close my eyes

I’m sound

I’m loud

II’m motion

I’m a calming notion

I’m trust

I’m lust

I’m fear

I’m a tear in your heart

I’m whatever I want to be

And anything I care to be

And when I open my eyes

What I care to be

is me.

the rain is nice, but

Interlude 1

For the next two poems, we’re taking a bit of a break. Well. Kinda. It’s not sarcasm, but I didn’t make them to be entirely serious either. They still have messages behind them, as all of my other poems always will.

“I’ll be there”

“ill be there for you”

means “when you need me, i leave”

and i learned the hard way.

“Raccoon”

Raccoon

Fuzzy, sneaky, possibly rabid

Gone in a flash

Near my trash can

No voice, no clothes, no remorse.

“The purpose of our lives is to be happy”

its hard to find the

reason to keep finding my purpose

in my life. but when i think of

her, and imagine what our

time spent together could do to our lives,

it makes it easier. i know then what my purpose is,

and explaining it would be too difficult to

put into words. and im not trying to be

emotional or deep, im explaining why im happy.

untitled - number 1

No more obsessions with the stats, the numbers didn’t call you

You don’t need Givenchy, you need Jesus

But you can’t expect me to respect you if you ain’t gonna give it yourself

I can’t love when I can’t even love myself

On the attack, the ignorant see me and they see food

No one really wants to die alone, but some of us will try

I’m sick of drowning, need a change of my surroundings

Am I the antichrist to you?

Why they always lookin’ at me? ’Cause they see what they never could be!

So when you ask, “Was it worth it?” Of course it was!

Art

Art

Music

Peaceful sound

A calm process

to produce something

that will be beautiful

with all of your unique sounds

so that the world will scream out loud:

“this music really is beautiful!”

interlude 2

this next poem i did for a poetry cafe that was being held at the school. i later used the same poem for a personal endeavour, however, i take great pride in this, as i wrote it completely from scratch, which was something i didnt think i could do. the meaning, to me, is powerful.

Hope.

Most days I struggle to find hope

But maybe, it's just because I'm not sure on how to cope

Ever since I went through it all,

I've been too scared to fall.

Through your beautiful hair and wonderful eyes,

It scares me to think of the surprise.

The surprise I'd get if you dropped me now,

and watch as I spiral down.

For you, though, I take the risk,

and hope, maybe one day, you'll show me bliss.

But I'm too scared, so I think I'll just wait,

So that I don't just show up point blank.

I can't say why, but I'm scared to ask you,

Is it the rejection I fear that I'll get when I step into view?

No, now's my time. I can't let this slip away.

I'm getting over all of these fears today.

Epilogue

for this anthology, i started with “By Myself” and ended with “Hope.” because i wanted this to show where i “started” and where it brought me. these poems aren’t in order from when i started writing until now, but they all make sense with each other. i wasnt trying to establish any pace, to be honest. i want you, as the reader, to take this in at your own pace and understanding. my title came from one of many philosophies that i believe in. no matter the occasion, it should never be about the destination. you miss out on so much by doing that. it always should be about the journey, as thats where you make the most memories. the poems dont necessarily share one theme, but thats why i chose them! i didnt want it to be stuck to one theme between the poems. some of these poems didnt have titles, so i had to think of them on the spot. im not very creative, so you can only imagine where “untitled - number 1” came from. that isnt to say that i wont have any poems that ill need to name “untitled”, but now it makes it easier for when i do have to do that. at the end of the day, i just wanted to show people the progress that i’ve made, and hope to one day inspire them.
